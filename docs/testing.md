# Testing and Validation

## Quality Assurance Methodology

CluckTalk follows a rigorous, iterative testing workflow. Rather than relying on informal manual checks, the development process utilizes a formal defect management strategy involving:

- **Functional & GUI Testing:** Systematic verification of UI elements and core features.
- **Compatibility Testing:** Validation across multiple device classes to ensure a consistent user experience.
- **Exploratory Testing:** Identification of edge cases in navigation and audio playback.

## Beta Testing Program

The application underwent a 14-day closed beta testing program involving a cohort of over 12 active testers and more than 12 real-world devices. Key evaluation areas included:

- **Feature Testing:** Functionality of the Learn, Search, and Quiz modules.
- **Performance Testing:** Monitoring app loading speed, response time, and stability.
- **Bug Tracking:** A centralized process was used to record and remediate over 20 documented defects, including navigation interruptions and audio playback inconsistencies.

## Reproducibility

The testing process was designed to ensure that CluckTalk functions as a mature, robust educational platform. By documenting these procedures, we provide evidence of the software's reliability and its readiness for educational deployment.
