## Geographic Distribution

CluckTalk has reached users across five continents, including:

**North America** · **Europe** · **Asia** · **Africa** · **Oceania**

Countries with documented installs include the United States, Canada, India, France, the Philippines, Germany, the United Kingdom, Australia, South Africa, and Iraq, among others.

---

## Interpretation

Deployment metrics reflect platform reach and sustained engagement rather than formal learning outcomes. The 789 MAU figure indicates ongoing use beyond initial installation, suggesting the application serves a recurring educational purpose across its international user base.

A formal pedagogical evaluation is identified as a direction for future work.

---

## Requirements

No internet connection is required after installation. No account, login, or cloud configuration is needed. The application runs entirely on-device and is compatible with any iOS or Android device meeting the minimum platform specifications listed in [`docs/installation.md`](installation.md).
